The solar system consists of the Sun and the astronomical objects bound to it by gravity. There are eight planets in total. Mercury is the smallest terrestrial planet. Venus is known for its thick, toxic atmosphere. Earth is the only known planet to harbor life. Mars, often called the Red Planet, has a thin atmosphere. Jupiter is a gas giant and the largest planet. Saturn is famous for its extensive ring system. Uranus and Neptune are the two ice giants in the outer solar system.
